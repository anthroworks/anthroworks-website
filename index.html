How We Work

The practice, in motion.

A small practice, supported by a considered stack of AI tools. The work — research, writing, product design, correspondence — stays in human hands; AI carries the rhythm around it so the thinking can stay deliberate.

This site was designed and built by hand, in dialogue with those tools. The research behind it is still being written. We favour depth over frequency, and we’re transparent about how AI sits in the work: as a medium, carefully chosen, never the centre.

This is the capability we bring to organisations — not AI for its own sake, but AI shaped by context and care.

The Approach

Observe. Participate. Re-imagine.

01

Observe

Every project begins with cultural research — anthropological fieldwork, indigenous knowledge systems, and deep attention to context. We listen and understand before we make.

02

Participate

We work with communities, not for them. Co-stewardship over ownership. Reciprocity over extraction. Ubuntu as architecture, not aesthetic.

03

Re-imagine

Research becomes practice. Practice becomes publications, products, and possibilities — new ways of seeing what was already there. Slow. Deliberate. Depth over frequency.
